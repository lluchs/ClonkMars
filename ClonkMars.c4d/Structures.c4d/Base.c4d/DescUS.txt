A save base with trading unit

With jumping you open the menu where you can get supply capsules. These land somewhere near or you build a capsule port.

You also can produce Clonks. While the breeding process is running, there must be always at least one Clonk, enough room, a green base and power.

If one of these requirements is not redeemed, the process will fail and start from the beginning. The process always starts automatic.
